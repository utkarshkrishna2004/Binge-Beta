Sloth is a unique and exciting hackathon that provides participants with a full 36 hours to build their own unique and innovative projects. The event is named after the slow-moving creature, as participants have ample time to take their time and build their ideas at their own pace.

Sloth is designed to encourage creativity and independence, with participants given the freedom to explore their own ideas and work on projects that are of interest to them. The hackathon is open to participants of all skill levels, from experienced developers to hobbyists and students, and provides a platform for all to showcase their skills and creativity.

Throughout the event, participants will be supported by a team of experienced mentors who will be on hand to offer guidance, advice, and technical expertise as needed. The focus is on innovation, with participants encouraged to push the boundaries of what is possible and develop cutting-edge solutions to real-world problems.

At the end of the hackathon, participants will have the opportunity to showcase their projects to a panel of judges who will evaluate submissions based on a range of criteria, including innovation, functionality, and usability. The top projects will be recognized and awarded prizes for their hard work and dedication.

Overall, Sloth promises to be an exciting and rewarding experience for all participants, providing a unique opportunity to explore new ideas, collaborate with others, and push the boundaries of what is possible. So get ready to join us for an unforgettable 36 hours of hacking, learning, and fun!

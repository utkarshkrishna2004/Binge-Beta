What is Binge?

Are you ready to binge on innovation? Binge is the perfect platform to indulge in a weekend of non-stop creativity, collaboration, and competition. Whether you're an experienced developer, or simply have a passion for problem-solving, the Binge platform is open to all. With challenges spanning a range of industries and domains, you'll have the chance to work on cutting-edge technology and make real impacts on the world. Not only will you have the opportunity to showcase your skills and network with fellow innovators, but you'll also have the chance to win exciting prizes and recognition for your hard work. So come join us for a weekend of coding, designing, and binging on all things innovation. Who knows, you might just create the next big thing!

How to Binge?

Browsing the available hackathons: Users can browse through the list of upcoming hackathons on Binge. They can filter by location, date, type of hackathon, and other criteria to find events that match their interests and availability.They'll need to provide some basic information such as their name, email address, and a password to create an account on Binge. Joining a team: After registering, users can join a team if they want to work with others on a project. They can either create their own team or join an existing one based on their interests and skills.Attending the hackathon: Users can attend the hackathon either in person or virtually, depending on the type of event.Overall, Binge is designed to provide a user-friendly platform for hackathon participants to register, join teams, attend events, and submit projects.
